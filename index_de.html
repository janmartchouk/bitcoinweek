About

The German Bitcoin Week is an international gathering of pioneers, researchers, coders, enthusiasts, investors and entrepreneurs coming together to spread Bitcoin knowledge. Whilst the money printers go brrrrrr, Bitcoiners will be congregating in the banking capital of Europe to advance the first distributed, permissionless, trustless and censorship-resistent network and digital asset through numerous meetups, workshops, talks and dinner parties. No event is too big and no party too small, as long as it's done in the spirit of the mother of all Blockchains: Bitcoin. Let's unite and praise Satoshi!

Events

  • Aug 27; Starts Thursday 11:00Bitcoin Week Start & Pre-Convention-Run with Team Satoshi

    As a fresh and healthy start into German Bitcoin Week and UNCHAIN Convention 2020 Team Satoshi invites you to join a fabulous community run. The run will be lead by a qualified coach from Bitcoin Runners.

  • Aug 27; Starts Thursday 16:00The Blockcamp - an unconference measuring the Bitcoin & blockchain world

    Be it market cap, liquidity, hash rate or anonymity set: the world of cryptocurrencies is quantified. This open-format event - hosted by Dr. Philipp Giese (Chief Analyst at BTC-ECHO for many years) - will introduce great milestones researchers achieved regarding the analysis of Bitcoin and other Blockchain-based projects, amongst others.

  • Aug 28-29; Starts Friday 22:00UNCHAIN Bitcoin & Blockchain Convention

    UNCHAIN Convention is a 2 day conference featuring leading Bitcoin and blockchain experts - debating the most exciting issues of Bitcoin and socio-economic impacts of blockchain real world use cases. In cooperation with Labitconf and Baltic Honeybadger Conference; voted as one of the Top10 Blockchain conferences worldwide 2018.

  • Aug 28; Starts Friday 8:00Friday-Run with Team Satoshi

    As warm-up for conference day one of UNCHAIN Convention Team Satoshi invites you to join an early bird community run. The run will be lead by a qualified coach from Bitcoin Runners.

  • Aug 29; Starts Saturday 8:00Saturday-Run with Team Satoshi

    As recovery from the evening and start into conference day two of UNCHAIN Convention Team Satoshi invites you to join another community run. Leaded by a qualified coach from Bitcoin Runners, nobody gets left behind!

Highlights

Bitcoin is a decentralised digital currency, giving high financial power and sovereignty back to the people. In this respect, Bitcoin Week is designed as a festival of decentralisation itself, by presenting a multitude of independent events - true to the motto "the more, the merrier". What's planned? You'll have the chance to expand your Bitcoin knowledge and network at every single one of the events. Some highlights include a workshop tackling the Lightning Network, a scaling solution that could bring Bitcoin to the masses; a workshop on how to circumvent surveillance by the very banksters that populate the great city of Frankfurt; arts and memes with the grandmaster of memetics; panel discussions between the roaring dinosaurs of financial legacy and the proponents of a new monetary freedom and individual sovereignty; last but not least as an opening concert of this festival, the third annual edition of the UNCHAIN Convention.
